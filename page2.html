MUSICAL TERMS

Here are all the definitions for the bolded terms of the analysis

1. augmentation

    the lengthening of a note/interval

2. conjunct  

   smooth; easy to sing or play

3. consonance

   a smooth-sounding harmonic combination

4. diminution

    the shortening of a note/interval 

5. disjunct

    disjointedly ragged or jumpy; difficult to sing or play

6. dissonance

    harsh-sounding harmonic combination

7. false cadence

    a false conclusion that creates a sense of resolution

8. homophonic

    the main melody accompanied by chords

9. monophonic

    single melodic line

10. polyphonic

    two or more melodic lines

11. prosody

    the patterns of rhythm and sound 

12. theme

     melody that is the basis for an extended musical work

13. tonality

    harmony that focuses on a "home" key center (opposite: atonality)
